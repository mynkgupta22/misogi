
Title: Overview of Common Medical Conditions and Their Management

1. Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)
- Definition: Persistent elevation of blood pressure above 130/80 mmHg
- Risk factors: Age, obesity, family history, high sodium intake
- Management: Lifestyle modifications, antihypertensive medications

2. Type 2 Diabetes
- Definition: Chronic condition affecting blood sugar regulation
- Symptoms: Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue
- Management: Diet control, exercise, oral medications, insulin therapy

3. Coronary Artery Disease
- Definition: Narrowing of coronary arteries due to plaque buildup
- Risk factors: Smoking, high cholesterol, hypertension
- Management: Lifestyle changes, medications, possible interventional procedures

4. Asthma
- Definition: Chronic inflammatory airway disease
- Symptoms: Wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness
- Management: Inhaled corticosteroids, bronchodilators, trigger avoidance

5. Osteoarthritis
- Definition: Degenerative joint disease
- Symptoms: Joint pain, stiffness, reduced range of motion
- Management: Physical therapy, pain management, weight loss if needed
