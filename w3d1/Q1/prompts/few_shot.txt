Example 1:  
Q: A bus covers 100 km in 2 hours. What is its speed?  
A: Speed = Distance / Time = 100 / 2 = 50 km/h

Example 2:  
Q: There are 3 boxes, each with 12 chocolates. How many chocolates are there in total?  
A: 3 × 12 = 36 chocolates
